                                Bheemeshwar Reddy A


 Degree:  Ph.D. Contact Details K-206, Assistant Professor,Department of Economics and Finance, Pilani
Hyderabad Campus
Jawahar Nagar, Kapra Mandal
Dist.-Medchal-500 078
Telangana, India
Phone: +91-40-66303648  About the Faculty  He has a Masters in Development Studies from Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Mumbai. He has carried out his Ph.D. at Economic Analysis Unit, Indian Statistical Institute, Bangalore. His research interests include labour economics, issues of income and educational inequality and demography. Currently, his research focuses on issues such as intergenerational mobility, employment and labour market discrimination.
