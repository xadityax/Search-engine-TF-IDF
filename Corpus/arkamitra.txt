Worked on cementitious systems containing supplementary cementitious materials. Bachelor of Civil Engineering from Jadavpur University, Kolkata, India in 2008. Honors and Achievements  2020 Technical Committee Member, RILEM TC MPA.2020 Certified Publons Academy Mentor, Practical Peer Review Training Course.2018 Certificate of Reviewing, Construction and Building Materials (ELSEVIER)2015 Valued Reviewer Recognition, Construction and Building Materials (ELSEVIER)2015 BITS OPERA Award.2014 International Conference on Advanced Materials Research and Application, Dec 12-14, 2014, Sanya, Hainan, China - Acknowledged Reviewer2013 - Proceedings of 28th Annual Technical Conference, September 9-11, 2013, Penn State University State College, PA - Acknowledgement for stereomicroscopy studies.2013 Graduated as Doctorate from WVU with 4.0 GPA2013 West Virginia University Dissertation Fellowship.2012 ASCE Freeman Fellowship.2012-13 Treasurer, ASCE - Structural Engineering Institute (SEI) at WVU.2011 Engineer-In-Training, West Virginia State Board of Registration for Professional Engineers.2011 ASCE Virginias' Conference, part of the judges' panel for the 'Concrete Bowling Competition' and assisted in the design of the 'Concrete Canoe' made by Team WVU.2009 PCI "Big Beam Contest", 4th Place in Zone-IV (along with Adam Justice, Matt Anderson, and Soumya Sundar Chowdhury).Professional Memberships Affiliate Member, RILEM (#36902)Associate Member, ASCE (#9135386)Faculty In-Charge, Civil Engineering Association at BITS-Pilani, Hyderabad CampusTreasurer, ASCE Structural Engineering Institute at
West Virginia University (2012-13)Life member, Institute of Civil Engineers, Jadavpur University, India.  Reviewer for Journal of Cleaner Production (ELSEVIER)Waste Management (ELSEVIER) Arabian Journal for Science and Engineering (SPRINGER)Advances in Civil Engineering (Hindawi)Construction and Building Materials (ELSEVIER)Scientia Iranica (erstwhile ELSEVIER, presently Sharif University, Iran) Journal of The Institution of Engineers (India): Series A (SPRINGER) Extra-curricular activities Volunteer, Association for India's Development (AID)Project Coordinator and mentor, 2011 - present (for Morgantown chapter) Chapter President, 2011Recognition Award for services, 2013. Online Collaborator, hitRECord.Mentored WVU's Concrete Canoe Team from 2010 - 2012 with the concrete mix designs.
