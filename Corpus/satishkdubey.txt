                                Dr. Satish Kumar Dubey


  Contact Details Assistant Professor, Department of Mechanical Engineering    Birla Institute of Technology & Science, Pilani Hyderabad Campus Jawahar Nagar, Shameerpet Mandal Hyderabad, 500078, Telangana State, India Phone: +91 040-66303 673 (office)Office: Room No. E-205 Email: satishdubey@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.in  Lab website: (www.mmne.in) [MEMS, Microfluidics and Nanoelectronics Lab]  UpdateFull-time PhD candidates with their own fellowship (UGC/CSIR JRF etc) are welcomed to contact. About the Faculty 
Dr. Satish Kumar Dubey, is Assistant Professor at Mechanical Engineering Department, BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus with qualification Ph.D. (BITS, Pilani), M. Tech. Thermal Engineering  (MANIT, Bhopal) and B.E. Mechanical Engineering (Pt. R.S.U. Raipur).His research interest include: Microfluidics, MEMS, Computational Heat Transfer, Simulation of Thermal Systems, Bio Heat Transfer, etc. Teaching interests are: MEMS, Heat Transfer, Applied Thermodynamics, Thermodynamics, I. C. Engines, Refrigeration & Air conditioning, Power Plant Engineering, Computational Heat Transfer and Fluid Dynamics, etc.  Professional MembershipMember of The Institutions of Engineers (India) IEI, Membership number: M-148101-5 Member of Indian Society of Heat and Mass Transfer( ISHMT), Membership number: 649Member of American Society of Mechanical Engineering (ASME), Membership number:1001897 Institute Responsibilities In charge (along with Dr. Souvik Kundu) Clean room : Micro- Nano Fabrication Facility, BITS Pilani, Hyderabad CampusNon Resident Warden, Hostel: Ram BhawanDRC Member Mechanical Engineering Department
