Sponsored Projects










                                Sponsored projects


"Does Financial Awareness lead to Better Financial Inclusion 
Outcomes? An Investigation", sponsored by ICSSR, New Delhi under the IMPRESS
 scheme. It is an ongoing project and is an action research in nature.  Some Interesting results: The project is now complete and we are analyzing the data. I am sharing some interesting results of our training: How the training has impacted the awareness about the PMJDY? How our training affected awareness about cashless transactions?  How our training affected the mode of cash withdrawals from bank account? How our training affected the preferred mode of saving surplus cash? Please feel free to contact if you want to know more about this project.
