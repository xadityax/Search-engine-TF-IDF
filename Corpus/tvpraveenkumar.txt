Seminars










                                Seminars




1) Presented a paper entitled “Shortest path routing algorithm based on modified de bruijn graphs” in International conference on recent developments in Statistics and their applications(I.C.R.D.A) held from 4-4 jan 2005 in department of statistics , SV University, Tirupathi.
 
2) Presented a paper entitled “comparison of three different logical topologies in multihop lightwave networks in national conference on recent trends in mathematical sciences” held from 31 july- 1st august in Department of Mathematics, Sri Krishnadevaraya University, Anantapur.
3) Participated in the workshop on ‘Matlab and Simulink’ held at The Icfai Institute of Science and Technology, Hyderabad during 22-24 December, 2008.
 
4) Participated in the “WORKSHOP ON STATISTICAL AND MATHEMATICAL METHODS IN BIOLOGY” during December 26, 2009 to December 29, 2009,  held at Department Biotechnology, Bioinformatics Infrastructure facility, University of Hyderabad. 5) Participated and Presented a Paper " Optimization of Culture Variables for the production of a Cytotoxic Yellow Pigment from Streptomyces Griseoaurantiacus JUACT01  Using Response Surface Methodology" at the International Conference ICMBAA-2015 held from June 4 to June 6, 2015, at the Department of Applied Mathematics, Aligarh Muslim University, Aligarh, India.
