                                Capsule Bio


After graduating from Jadavpur University in 2006, Surya S. Dan joined the Indian Institute of Science Bangalore, India, where he earned his PhD in 2009. During his PhD he had developed a compact analytical model for single electron transistor devices which describes the quantum effects influencing the behavior of single electron transistor devices and circuits at the nanoscale regime. After his PhD he had worked as a Scientist at the Swiss Federal Institute of Technology, Switzerland, where he developed a methodology to accurately determine the lateral junction steepness of a tunnel field effect transistor. In 2012 he joined as an Assistant Professor in the Dept. of Electrical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad (IITH). Thereafter, he joined as an Assistant Professor in the Dept. of Electronics & Electrical Communication Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (IITKGP) in 2013. Since 2016, he is with the Dept. of Electronics and Electrical Engineering, Birla Institute of Technology & Science (BITS) Pilani, Hyderabad Campus. Since 2012 he is pursuing the development of physics based compact analytical model for the state-of-the-art tunnel field effect transistor devices.
