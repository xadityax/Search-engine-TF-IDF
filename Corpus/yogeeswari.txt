About the Faculty
 

Qualification


Ph. D. (Medicinal Chemistry), Institute of Technology, Banaras Hindu University, Varanasi, India, 2001.
Master of Pharmacy (Medicinal Chemistry), Banaras Hindu University, India, 1998.
Bachelor of Pharmacy, The Tamilnadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University, Chennai, India, 1996 (State Topper, Gold medalist).



 
Awards:

Best Paper in Podium presentation was awarded in the 11th APTI national Convention held in September 1-3, 2006 at Bangalore for the paper entitled "Synthesis of 4-(1,3-dioxo-1,3-dihydro-2H-isoindol-2-yl)-N-(substituted phenyl)butanamides possessing antiallodynic and antihyperalgesic activities in two rat models of neuropathic pain".
