                                Prof. Ramesh Babu Adusumalli


Associate Professor, Dept. of Chemical Engineering Birla Institute of Technology & Science, Pilani Hyderabad Campus Jawahar Nagar, Kapra Mandal Medchal District - 500 078; Telangana, India. Tel: +91-40-66303-554 or Mobile: +91-9848723783   ABOUT THE FACULTY:   Aug. 2010 - till date: Working at BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus   Feb. 2008 – Jan. 2010: Post-doctoral fellow, Switzerland; Mechanics of Materials and Nanostructures Laboratory; EMPA (ETH Zurich)                                                            Jan. 2005 - Dec. 2007: Ph.D. in Cellulose Fibre Reinforced Composites; University of Natural Resources and Applied Life Sciences (BOKU), Vienna, Austria; Thesis: Characterisation of Cellulose Fibres & Composites   Sep. 2002 - Sep. 2004: Post-graduate Research Assistant, Germany; Institute for Composite Materials, TU Chemnitz                     Sep. 2000 – Feb. 2002: Master of Science in Biobased Materials; University of Applied Sciences, Reutlingen, Germany   1996 - 2000: Bachelor of Technology in Chemical Engineering, C.B.I.T College (Affiliated to Osmania University) Hyderabad, India   PUBLICATIONS:    1. SCOPUS Indexed Journals - 25 2. Book Chapters  - 02 4. Conference Proceedings - 20    AWARDS:   1. Received the Best Oral Presentation Award for the paper << Tensile testing of Single Fibres>> presented at Second International Conference on Structural Integrity and Exhibition (SICE 2018, 25-27.07.2018, Hyderabad) under the category "delegates".   2. Received the 1st Runner up Award for the paper << The Influence of Super Masscolloider and Lab Valley beater on the Morphology of Sapwood Pulp Fibres>> presented at IPPTA Workshop cum Seminar, Rajahmundry 8-9th Nov, 2019.     ADMINISTRATIVE ROLES:   1. Faculty-in-Charge: Centralized Purchases Unit (CPU) (Aug. 2018 -   )   2. Head of the Department: Aug. 2014 - Sept. 2016     3. Organizing secretary – AMDA 2014: National Conference on Advanced Materials for Defence and Aerospace Applications, Dec. 22, 2014 was organised at BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus. Bharat Ratna Prof. C.N. R Rao was the Keynote Speaker.   4. Convener – ICE 2013 : National conference on Innovations in Chemical Engineering ICE 2013, Nov. 15-16, 2013 was organised as Convener at BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus. Total 180 participants were attended from 8 different states.    5. Organizing secretary – WEMEP 2012: Indo-Danish International Conference on Wind Energy: Materials, Engineering and Policies, WEMEP 2012, Nov. 22-23, 2012 was organised at BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus. Total 150 delegates were participated.      MEMBERSHIP IN ACADEMIC BODIES:   1. Member in Board of studies for Chemical Engineering Department, Chaitanya Bharathi Institute of Technlogy (CBIT), Gandipet, Hyderabad (autonomous)   2. Life Member (LM-53128), Indian Institute of Chemical Engineers (IIChE)   3. Member (CPPRI-IM: 704), Central Pulp and Paper Research Institute, Saharanpur, U.P
