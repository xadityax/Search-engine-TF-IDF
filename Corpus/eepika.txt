Profile










                                Dr. Deepika


Contact Details:Office Room No.: H032Department of MathematicsBirla Institute of Technology & Science, PilaniHyderabad Campus Jawahar Nagar, Kapra Mandal, Medchal Dist.Telangana - 500078  Email: deepika(at)hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.inPhone: 040-66303589 About the Faculty:Dr. Deepika works as an Assistant Professor at the Department of Mathematics, BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus. Prior to joining BITS, she was a postdoctoral fellow at National Institute of Science Education and Research(NISER), Bhubaneswar. She completed her Ph.D. from IIT Kanpur in the year 2017 under the supervision of Prof. Manjul Gupta. Her research interests are Functional Analysis and Operator Theory.
