Profile










                                Dr Komaragiri Srinivasa Raju, Professor



   Educational Qualifications Post-Doctoral Scientist, joint research programme of ENGREF (École nationale du génie rural, des eaux et des forêts) & LAMSADE (Laboratoire d'Analyse et de Modélisation des Systèmes pour l'Aide à la Décision), University of Paris-Dauphine, Paris, France during February–July 2000 under the supervision of Prof. Lucien Duckstein in the field of multi-criterion decision making with reference to water resources planning.Ph.D (water resources engineering) civil engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur, India in August 1996 under the guidance of Prof C.R.S.Pillai. Title of Study is ‘Studies on multicriterion decision making methods and management of irrigation systems (Dissertation is appreciated by experts as contribution to the field of multicriterion decision making)M.Tech (water resources engineering) civil engineering from Indian Institute of Technology, Madras, India in March 1991. B.E  (civil engineering) from Acharya Nagarjuna University, Vijayawada, India in 1987.     Research Interests Impact of climate change on water resources Water resources systemsMultiobjective optimization and decision making  Soft computing and evolutionary algorithms   View my brief résumé  New Book on Fluid Mechanics by K.Srinivasa Raju and D.Nagesh Kumar   K.Srinivasa Raju and D.Nagesh Kumar (2020) Fluid Mechanics: Problem solving using MATLAB, Prentice Hall of India (PHI), New Delhi, ISBN-978-93-89347-63-0 (Book brochure) (Book cover page) (PHI homepage link for the book) (Review of the book by Prof VP Singh, Texas A & M, USA) (Review by Prof CSP Ojha, IIT Roorkee in ISH News Bulletin July 2020)   Contact Details:
 
#D107, Department of Civil EngineeringBirla Institute of Technology & Science, Pilani - Hyderabad CampusJawahar Nagar, Kapra MandalMedchal District - 500 078Telangana, India  Email: ksraju[at]hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.in Phone: 91-40-6630 3519
