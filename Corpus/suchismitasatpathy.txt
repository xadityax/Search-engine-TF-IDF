Profile










                                Dr. Suchismita Satpathy


DegreePh.DContact Details K- 126, Department of Humanities and Social SciencesBITS Pilani, Hyderabad CampusJawahar Nagar, Shameerpet, Kapra MandalHyderabad, Medchal District. Telangana State- 500078, India Email: suchi[AT]hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.in Phone: 040 - 66303631 About the FacultySuchismita Satpathy is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Humanities and Social Science, at BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus. She has been a visiting faculty at the Centre for Public Policy and Governance at TISS Hyderabad RM campus during June 2016- April 2017. Apart from this, she was associated with the UGC e-pg pathshala Philosophy project, Department of Philosophy, University of Hyderabad. It produces online course material for the M.A. Philosophy students. Her interest lies in various aspects of urban sociology. Research InterestThe political economy of urban spaceCorporate social responsibility SegregationSmart cities and local bodiesRight to city and citizenship
