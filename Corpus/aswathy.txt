                                Aswathy Raveendran


Aswathy Raveendran has a PhD in Science Education, from the Homi Bhabha Centre for Science Education, Tata Institute of Fundametal Research. Her research interests include Science and Technology Studies, Feminist Science Studies and Philosophy of Science.  Contact details: email: aswathy@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.inOffice phone no: 040-66203923
