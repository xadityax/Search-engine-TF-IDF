Profile










                                Dr. Sumit Kumar Chatterjee


Ph.D (IIT, Kharagpur)Contact Details Sumit K Chatterjee, Assistant ProfessorRoom No-D-328, EEE DepartmentBITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus, 500078 Email: sumit2702@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.inPhone: 040-66303671About the FacultySumit K Chatterjee is working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering at BITS Pilani (Hyderabad), India. Before joining to BITS Pilani, he has worked at ECE Department,  NIT Sikkim (an institute of national importance), Sikkim, India as the Faculty in Charge (Head) of the Department. He has more than ten years of teaching and research experience in various areas of digital VLSI design. His research area includes digital VLSI design, video compression and anti blocking filter design. He has also co-authored one book with in the area of Video compression published by Springer, Germany.
