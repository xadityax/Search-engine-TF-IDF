Broad Research interestsStructure function relationship in macromolecular assemblies including DNA, proteins and ligands, both under normal and diseased condition Specific research topics1. Use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) to explore, annotate and predict communicable diseases, like COVID-192. Reactive Oxygen Species generation, scavenging and involvement in diseases 3. Medical Bioinformatics of H1N1 influenza virus4.  Protein microenvironment an important measure to elucidate functions of amino acids related to different biochemical pathways5. Annotation and prediction of various cysteine functions based on their protein microenvironments 6. Biomemetics study to combine robustness of chemo-catalyst and specificity of bio-catalyst to enhance yield from a reaction
