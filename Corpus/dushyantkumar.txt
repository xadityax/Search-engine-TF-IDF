Profile










                                Dr. Dushyant Kumar


PhD (Quantitative Economics), Indian Statistical Institute I am an Assistant Professor at the Department of Economics and Finance of the BITS Pilani, Hyderabad campus. Before joining BITS, I was a post-doctoral fellow at the Centre for Development Economics of the Delhi School of Economics (DSE). I have completed my Ph.D from Indian Statistical Institute, Delhi Centre in October, 2015. I am an applied theorist, by training and research interest. Currently I am working in the areas of conflict theory and, organisation theory. Contact Details K-223, Department of Economics and FinanceBITS Pilani, Hyderabad CampusJawahar Nagar, Shameerpet Mandal,Hyderabad, 500078 Email: dushyant@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.inPhone: 040-66303711
