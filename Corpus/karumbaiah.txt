                                Karumbaiah Chappanda Nanaiah


Full time PhD students (with their own scholarship) who are interested to work in the field of micro-plasma devices, bio/chemical/gas sensors,  photo-catalytic water splitting, super capacitors  are encouraged to contact.  Contact details:E-mail: karumcn@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.inPhone: (O)+91 4066303577 (M) +91-7568520721Office:H-225Address: Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering;        Birla Institute of Technology and Science Pilani, Hyderabad Campus;        Jawahar Nagar; Shameerpet  Mandal; Ranga Reddy Dist.;         Secunderabad; Telangana State - 500078 About FacultyDr. Karumbaiah Chappanda Nanaiah (Karum) is currently working as an assistant professor in department of electronics and electrical engineering department at BITS-Pilani, Hyderabad campus. After his PhD, he worked at BITS-Pilani, Pilani campus as assistant professor for 10 months (2013-2014). He then worked as a post-doctoral fellow at KAUST, Saudi Arabia for 4 years (2014-2018). During his post-doc, he also a full time visiting scholar at Massachusetts Institute of Technology during Jan 2018. His current research interest is in MEMS, Sensors, and device fabrication.Educational qualificationPhD: University of Utah- 2013MS: Michigan Technological University - 2010BE: Visvesvaraya Technological University -2007
