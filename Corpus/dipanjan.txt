                                Dipanjan Chakraborty


PhD, IIT Delhidipanjan@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.inOffice: H131  I joined as an Assistant Professor at BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus, in the Department of Computer Science and Information Systems, in July 2019. Previously I was a Post Doctoral Researcher at Gram Vaani. I have a PhD from the School of IT at IIT Delhi in the area of Information and Communication Technologies and Development (ICTD) and Human Computer Interaction (HCI). My older webpage is here

                                Research Interest


My research interest lies in the interdisciplinary field of ICTD (Information and Communication Technologies and Development) and HCI (Human Computer Interaction). Specifically, I look for broken or missing information flows, which, if present, would streamline the lives of marginalised people. Under this broad theme I have worked on voice-based information systems for text-illiterate users under several themes like employment, social security, health and nutrition, labour rights, etc. There are several technical problems to solve in order to solve the bigger problems listed above, including Speech to Text, Topic Modelling, Information Retrieval, IVR Engineering and Systems Engineering. If one is interested to know more about these topics, please drop me an email.
