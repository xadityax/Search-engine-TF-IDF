Profile










                                Dr. Prasant Kumar Pattnaik


Contact Details Assistant ProfessorDepartment of Electrical EngineeringBITS Pilani Hyderabad CampusJawaharnagar, Shameerpet(M)Hyderabad- 5000078 , Telengana. Phone: 040-66303612 Email: pkpattnaik@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.in About the Faculty Dr. Prasant Kumar Pattnaik is working as an Assistant Professor at department of Electrical Engineering, BITS Pilani Hyderabad campus since June 2012. He has three years of teaching and fifteen years of research experience. Prior to  joining BITS he was a Research Associate at ECE department, IISc Bangalore where he had done his Ph.D in the areas of Optical MEMS. He had also worked at Tata Elxsi Ltd., Bangalore as a Specialist Design and Development where he had filed four Indian patents in the  areas of Optical Networking, multimedia communication and embedded devices. He has published about 35 papers in national and international conferences and journals. His present research interest are in the areas of Photonics, Optical Communications, Photonic Integrated Circuits, and MEMS. Qualification Ph.D (ECE), IISc BangaloreM.Tech (Applied Optics: Fiber Optics and Digital Image Processing), Sri Sathya Sai Institute of Higher Learning, PrasanthinlayamM.Sc(Physics), Sri Sathya Sai Institute of Higher Learning, Prasanthinlayam
