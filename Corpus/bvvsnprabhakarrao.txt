                                Dr. BVVSN Prabhakar Rao


Contact Details ASSOCIATE  PROFESSOR,  Department of Electrical Engineering, BITS, PILANI - HYDERABAD CAMPUS, JAWAHAR NAGAR, SHAMEERPET MANDAL, R.R.DIST. HYDERABAD - 500078. (INDIA)  Email:   budhiraju@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.in; Phone: Office : 040-66303544About the Faculty Dr. Prabhakara Rao is working as Associate Professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering, BITS-Pilani, Hyderabad campus. He has 20 years of teaching and research experience. His current research interest is in the area of  battery management system / biomedical signal / image Processing.  Qualification : PhD (Nonlinear Optics), Indian Institute of Technology, DelhiM.Tech (Applied Optics), Indian Institute of Technology, DelhiMSc(Physics) Andhra University, Visakhapatnam
