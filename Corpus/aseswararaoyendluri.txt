                                Dr.YVD Rao


Dr. YENDLURI VENKATA DASESWARA RAO
 
 
Head Department of Mechanical Engineering, BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus (+91 40 66303551) till Sept 2016
 
Faculty In charge Maintenance Division (+91 40 66303829) till Sept 2016
 
Faculty In charge Engineering Services Division (+91 40 66303825)
 
Room:  E 108, Mechanical Engineering Department, 
 
BITS Pilani Hyderabad Campus, Jawaharnagar, Shameerpet, 
 
Hyderabad- 500078, Telangana State, India.
 
email: yvdrao@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.in
 
Educational Qualifications
 
1.         Ph.D. in Mechanical Engineering from Govt. College of Engineering, Raipur (NIT Raipur), Pt. Ravi Shankar Shukla University, Raipur in 2005. 
 
Topic of Thesis: “Synthesis and Analysis of Epicyclic Gear Trains”.
 
2.         M.Tech. in Design Engineering from IIT Delhi in 1984. CGPA:8.69
 
3.         B.Tech. in Mechanical Engineering from KREC, Surathkal ( NIT, Surathkal) in 1982. 75% First class with distinction.
 
 Academic EXPERIENCE: 
 


Was Head of Mechanical Engineering Department, Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani- Hyderabad Campus, Jawahar nagar, Shameerpet (M), Hyderabad from 1st January 2013 to Sept 2016.


Working as Professor in Mechanical Engineering Department of Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani- Hyderabad Campus, Jawahar nagar, Shameerpet (M), Hyderabad from 29th January 2010 to date.


Worked as Director of Sri Chaitanya College of Engineering and Technology, Sheriguda (v), Ibrahimpatnam (M),Hyderabad, India from 1st June 09 to Dec 2009.


Worked as Principal of Mahaveer Institute of Science and Technology, Hyderabad, India from 2nd August 07 to 30th May 09.


Worked as Principal of Vignan Institute of Technology and Science, Deshmukhi, India from 16th June 2006 to 1st August 07.


Promoted as Professor in Mechanical Engineering Department of Vignan Institute of Technology and Science, Deshmukhi, India with effect from 1st April 2006.


In- Charge Principal for Vignan Institute of Technology and Science, Deshmukhi, India with effect from 29th December 2004 to 3rd April 2005. 


Appointed as Vice-Principal of Vignan Institute of Technology and Science, Deshmukhi, India with effect from 29th December 2004 to 16th June 2006. 


Appointed Head of Mechanical Engineering Department, Vignan Institute of Technology and Science, Deshmukhi, India with effect from 28th, December 2003.


Associate Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engg of Vignan Institute of Technology and Science, Deshmukhi, Nalgonda (Dist.) India since 8th April, 2003 to 31st March, 06.


Worked as Assistant Professor in the Department of Mechanical Engg. of K.L. College of Engineering, Vaddeswaram, India from May 1996 to 7th April, 2003.


Worked as lecturer in the Department of Mechanical Engg. of V.R. Siddhartha Engg. College, Vijayawada, India from September 1985 to April 1996.


 
 Administrative EXPERIENCE:
 


Senate member of Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani, from 1st August 2010 to date.


Examination Committee member of Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani, from 1st August 2010 to 1st April 2013.


Purchase committee member of Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani, from 1st November 2010 to date.


Member of staff 


Faculty-in-charge of Engineering Hardware Division, Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani- Hyderabad Campus, Jawahar nagar, Shameerpet (M), Hyderabad from 1st November 2011 to 2016.


Faculty-in-charge of Engineering Services Division, Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani- Hyderabad Campus, Jawahar nagar, Shameerpet (M), Hyderabad from 1st August 2010 to date.


Faculty-in-charge of Maintenance Division, Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani- Hyderabad Campus, Jawahar nagar, Shameerpet (M), Hyderabad from 1st November 2011 to Sept 2016.


Faculty-in-charge of Transportation Division, Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani- Hyderabad Campus, Jawahar nagar, Shameerpet (M), Hyderabad from 1st November 2011 to August 2012.


Appointed as Chief Superintendent of examinations of Vignan Institute of Technology and Science, Deshmukhi, with effect from 18th April 2005 for the first semester JNT university examinations for 2004-05 academic year and first year annual JNT university examinations of academic Year 2004-05.


Responsible as Management Representative for auditing of ISO 9001: 2000 certifications of VITS, Deshmukhi done on 1st, April 2005. Certificate was renewed for one year after auditing by the certifying agency. 


Participated as member of internal Audit Team (External) of Gandhi Institute of Engineering & Technology, Gunupur- Orissa from 14th to 16th October 2004.


Nominated as a member of college academic committee, Vignan Institute of Technology and Science, Deshmukhi with effective from 21st August 2003.


Nominated as a member of college Disciplinary committee, Vignan Institute of Technology and Science, Deshmukhi with effective from 22nd September 2003.


Nagarjuna University Observer for II/IV B.Tech. Examinations from 21st August to 8th September 1995at K.L.C.E, Vaddeswaram


 
 
 
 
 
MEMBERSHIPS and PROFESSIONAL BODIES:
 
1.       Member of American Society for Mechanical Engineering, USA.
 
2.       Fellow of Institute of Engineers (IND), Kolkata, India. 
 
3.       Life Member of Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE), New Delhi.
 
4.       Life Member of Association of Mechanisms and Machines, India.
 
5.       Secretary cum Treasurer ISTE- KLCE Chapter, Vaddeswaram from June 1996 to December 2002.
 
6.       Advisor for ISTE- Student Chapter, KLCE, Vaddeswaram from March 1998 to March 2003.
 
7.       Secretary cum Treasurer ISTE- VITS Chapter, Deshmukhi from April 2003.
 
8.       President of ISTE- MIST Chapter, Mahaveer Institute of Science and Technology, Hyderabad from 30th August 07 to 25th May 09.
 
 
 
 
 
WORKSHOPS AND SEMINARS
 
01. Participated a six-day Workshop on “Pedagogy & class room teaching” held at V.R.Siddhartha Engg, College during 24th and 29 April 1989.
 
02. Participated in ISTE sponsored Three- Day Workshop on “Production Drawing Practice” under Canada-India special Assistance program held at Osmania University during 20th and 22nd 1993.
 
03.  Invited as judge for paper contest in Design stream as a part of National Level Symposium on Recent Trends in Mechanical Engineering – TECHNOHEIGHTS-2003, ON March 8, 2003 at Vignan Institute of Technology and Science, Deshmukhi.
 
04. Organized one day seminar on “Total Engineering Education quality Management” at Vignan Institute of Technology and Science, Deshmukhi, Nalgonda (Dist.) on 16th May, 2003 in association with ISTE – AP section, in which a good number of principals of different colleges participated. 
 
05. Conducted one day National Level Symposium on Recent Trends in Mechanical Engineering – TECHNOHEIGHTS-2004, ON March 5, 2004.
 
06. Attended a 34th Annual Convention of ISTE and National Seminar held at Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology, Dwaraka New Delhi during 18th and 20th December 2004.
 
07. Conducted Workshop on “Fatigue and Fracture behaviour of Engineering Materials” held during 6th and 7th January 2005 as president of the committee.
 
08. Organized one day National Level Symposium on Recent Trends in Mechanical Engineering – TECHNOHEIGHTS-2005, ON 12TH, March 2005 as President of the Symposium.
 
09. President of National level conference on “Applications of Mathematical Sciences for Engineers” was organized during 19th and 20th March 2005 at Vignan Institute of Technology and Science, Deshmukhi 
 
10.  Conducted ISTE sponsored Two- Day National Workshop on “Finite Element Methods” during 3rd and 4th December 2005 as president of the Workshop.
 
11.  Participated in National Convention on “Multi Layer Industry Institution Interaction in the global Context” conducted by ISTE, AP Section held at JNT University during 7th and 8th January 2006.
 
12.  Participated in National seminar on “Intellectual Property Rights – Academic & Technological Dimensions (An Indian Perspective)” sponsored by the Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India, New Delhi, held at JNT University on11th January 2006.
 
13. Gave thirteen minutes Technical talk on “Business trends and Outs Sourcing” in All India Radio, Hyderabad on 9th November 2006.
 
14. Invited as Judge for Mechanical Engineering paper presentation in “TIDES-2006” at Nizam Institute of Engineering and Technology, Deshmukhi, Nalgonda (Dt) during 20th and 21st February 2006.
 
15. Invited as External Judge for “INNOVATIONS 2006” at Gurunanak Engineering College, Ibrahimpatnam, and Hyderabad on March 16, 2006.
 
16. Invited as Subject expert of interview board for faculty selection at TKR college of Engineering and Technology, Meerpet, Hyderabad on 23rd May 2006. 
 
17. One Ph.D. Evaluation in KL University Vaddeswaram, Guntur (Dist), AP, India in March 2014.
 
18. One Ph.D. Evaluation at SWINBURNE UNIVERSITY OF TECHNOLOGY Australia in Feb 2014.
 
19. One Ph.D. Evaluation in Rayalaseema University, Kurnool, AP, India in March 2012.

20. Member of Departmental Advisory Committee of Mechanical Engineering Department at Vasavi College of Engineering, Hyderabad, Telangana in 2015-16.

21.Conducted one Day Workshop on "Hands-on-Microcontroller" on November 6, 2016 along with Dr.Arshad Javed of BITS Hyderbad.

22.  Organised as chairman of "3-Day Workshop on Computational Fluid Dynamics" on August 11-13, 2016.

23. Organised as chairman of "2nd National Conference on Design and Manufacturing Technologies for Product Life Cycle" on March 19-20, 2016.

24. Organised as chairman of "One Day Workshop on Material Characterization using Electron Back-Scatter Diffraction (EBSD) Technique" on March 14, 2016.

25. Organised as chairman of "International Symposium on Wind Engineering and Wind Energy" on January 28, 2016.

26. Organising member of "5th International conference on Product Modeling, Simulation and Synthesis" on December 15-17, 2015 and co-authored a poster which got the best poster awrd including cash award.

27. Organised as chairman of "Workshop on Innovations in Waste, Water and Energy Technologies for Rural Development" on July 13-14, 2015.

28. Member Board of Studies for Gayathree Vidyaparishad College of Engineering, Visakhapatnam, AP since August 2017.
