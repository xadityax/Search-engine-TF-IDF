Contact Details K 127, BITS Pilani, Hyderabad CampusJawahar Nagar, Shameerpet MandalHyderabad- 500078Email:  santosh@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.in Phone: 040 - 66303689 Fax: +91 40 66303998 About the Faculty Member Santosh Mahapatra has completed his Ph. D. in English Language Teaching at the Department of English, University of Hyderabad. Before joining BITS-Pilani, Hyderabad, he taught MCA and Integrated Masters students at the University of Hyderabad and worked as a Teaching Assistant at the English and Foreign Languages University, Hyderabad. His current research interests are tracer studies related to ESL teacher development in technology integration and classroom assessment, and critical analyses of English language education policies and practices. Educational Qualification Ph. D. in English, with Language Assessment Literacy as the area of specialization, at the University of Hyderabad (2015)M. Phil. in English Language Education, with English for Science and Technology as the area of specialization, at EFLU, Hyderabad (2010)   Institutional Responsibilities Nucleus member of the Teaching Learning Centre, BITS Pilani Hyderabad CampusConvener,  Departmental Committee for AcademicsMember of the editorial team for BITSCANSPOC for PS Division Warden, Married Research Scholars Tower (H7)Faculty-in-ChargeBITSMUNGrandhMovie Club
