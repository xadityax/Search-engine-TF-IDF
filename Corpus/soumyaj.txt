Profile










                                Dr. Soumya J


Full-time PhD candidates with their own fellowship/Part-time PhD candidates working in National Research Labs or Industry may be considered Proposed Research Area:Network-on-Chip Design Nature of work: Includes design and development of algorithms for different optimization problems in Network-on-Chip Design. Students with a working knowledge of any high level language and sound background on Digital Design are encouraged to mail me with their detailed CV.  Institutional Responsibilities  Faculty In-Charge, Placements(Campus wide): From August 2020 till date Purchase Committee Member (Campus wide): From November 2019 till dateDepartmental Research Committee Member: From December 2018 till date Degree Ph.D (IIT Kharagpur), M.Tech (IIT Kharagpur),  B.Tech (JNTU Hyderabad) Professional Experience August 2015 - Present:  Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering, BITS-Pilani, Hyderabad Campus, India  June 2015 - July 2015: Visiting Faculty in the Department of Electrical & Electronics Engineering, BITS-Pilani, Hyderabad Campus, India  December 2014 - May 2015: Faculty in the Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering, NIT Goa, Goa, India  September 2011 - June 2012: Scientist 'SC', Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO), Bangalore, India  Contact Details Dr. Soumya J, Assistant ProfessorRoom No: H237, EEE DepartmentBITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus, 500078  Email: soumyaj@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.in            soumyatkgp@gmail.com Phone:+914066303690
