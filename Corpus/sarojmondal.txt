Profile










                                Dr. Saroj Mondal


Educational Qualifications:  Ph.D., Department of Electronics and Electrical Engineering, IIT GuwahatiM.Tech., Institute of Radiophysics and Electronics, University of Calcutta B.Tech., Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, West Bengal University of Technology  Professional Experience:          Assistant Professor, BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus (current position)  Assistant Professor, Siliguri Institute of TechnologyTrainee, Transwitch India Pvt. Ltd.    Contact Details:          Room No: D321 Department of Electrical and Electronics EngineeringBITS Pilani, Hyderabad CampusJawahar Nagar, Shameerpet MandalHyderabad - 500078, Telangana, India Email: saroj@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.in/saroj.rpe@gmail.com     About the Faculty:           Saroj Mondal is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, BITS Pilani (Hyderabad Campus). He received his B.Tech. in Electronics and Communication Engineering from the West Bengal University of Technology, in 2007, M.Tech in VLSI Desing from Institute of Radiophysics and Electronics, University of Calcutta, in 2009, and Ph.D. in Electronics and Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati, in 2017. From 2009 to 2011, he was with the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering, Siliguri Institute of Technology, as an Assistant Professor. His research interests include micro-scale energy processing circuits and low power dc-dc converters.

Research Interest










                                Research Interest


VLSI Circuits and Systems:Mostly, working in the area of micro-scale power management interface circuits for IoT or Sensor nodes.  Expertise: On-chip DC-DC converters (both linear and switched capacitor), reference generator (current and voltage), MPPT circuits, RF - DC converters, matching networks, and start-up circuits.
