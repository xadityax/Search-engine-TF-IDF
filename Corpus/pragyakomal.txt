Research Interest










                                Research Interest


ResearchThe lab will focus on three broad topics namely:  (1)Immuno-neural interaction in neurodegenerative disorders and infectious disease model  A major interest in the lab is to examine the function of immune proteins in brain originally thought to be restricted in expression only on peripheral immune cells like the T-lymphocytes. We are also interested to explore how neurotransmitter gated ion-channels dysregulation impacts peripheral immune function.    (2) Exploit up-conversion nanoparticles (UCNPs) for MRI imaging and neural stimulation in neuropsychiatric disorders like schizophrenia Brain neural activity is severely affected during neurological disorders and with the help of lanthanide based nanoparticles we will aim to utilize upconversion nanoparticles to allow delivery of visible light deep into the brain to stimulate neural activities in non-invasive manner. This study will undertake an upconversion based strategy for combinatorial neural stimulation both in vitro and in vivo by using spectrum-selective upconversion nanoparticles (UCNPs).  (3) Impact of nicotine consumption during pregnancy and the risk of developing neuropsychiatric disorders in the offspring Smoking is one leading cause of mortality in India and worldwide. Apart from regular smokers, a big population in India are women and children who become passive smokers. Gradual intake of nicotine by pregnant mothers and children, can increase the chances in the offspring of developing neuropsychiatric disorders later in life. Nicotine is the alkaloid found in tobacco smoke and activates a group of protein receptors in the brain called nicotinic acetylcholine receptors (nAChRs). We want to explore if there is any correlation between the cholinergic receptor expression and development of neuropsychiatric disorders.
