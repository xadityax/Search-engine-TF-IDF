Courses










                                Courses



Distributed Data Systems Semester 1, 2020-2021

Data Mining Semester 2, 2019-2020

This is a CS open elective covering a range of topics including introduction to data mining, data preprocessing, data mining tasks including association rule mining, clustering, classification and anomaly detection. This course is ideal for students who want to get exposed to the exciting area of data science and get hands on experience of research oriented projects based on real world datasets.

Computer Networks Laboratory and Tutorial Semester 2, 2019-2020
Distributed Data Systems Semester 1, 2019-2020 

This is a Masters level module covering a range of topics including distributed databases, distributed file systems, big data storage systems, web data management, mobile databases. This is a very interesting course for those who are looking to gain more insight into how large scale data intensive systems and applications work in practice.


In the past, I have also taught modules on Fundamentals of Software development, Machine Learning, Sensors and Internet of Things.

Research Interest










                                Research Interests


I carry out research in the broad area of networks and data science to build real world systems and applications with social impact. Some of the key research themes am currently involved in are:Data collection and analysis for environmental monitoring (air and noise pollution), smart buildings (energy management) and mental health applicationsInternet of Things (IoT) analytics and distributed edge processingMachine learning techniques for sense-making from multimodal sensor data streams (time series, audio, text)
