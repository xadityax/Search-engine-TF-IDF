                                Dr. Kurra Suresh


M.Tech. (IITD), Ph.D (BITS) Contact Details Department of Mechanical EngineeringBirla Institute of Technology & Science, PilaniHyderabad Campus, Jawahar NagarShamirpeet Mandal, Ranga Reddy DistSecunderabad, Andhra Pradesh - 500078  Email: ksuresh@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.inPhone: 040-66303570 About Faculty  Education:  PhD - Mechanical Engineering, BITS Pilani – Hyderabad Campus, India. Thesis: Finite Element Studies and Experimental Validation of Asymmetric Incremental Sheet Forming of Extra Deep Drawn Steels.   MTech - Production Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IITD), India, May 2005. Thesis: Wear modeling of Hard turning using Ceramic tools. (CGPA: 8.6)   BE - Mechanical Engineering, Andhra University, India, May 2002. (74.62%)   Professional
Experience:Assistant Professor, Department of Mechanical Engineering, BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus, India.               December 2015 - Till date. Lecturer, Department of Mechanical Engineering, BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus, India.              August 2009 – December 2015.  Assistant Professor, JNTU affliated engineering colleges, Hyderabad, India.  August 2005 – August 2009.   Teaching Assistant, Indian Institute of Technology Delhi (IITD), India. May, 2003 – May 2005. Administrative Responsibilities: Non-Residential Warden, Vyasa Bhawan, BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus.(2010-July 2018) Nucleus Member, Central Workshop Unit, BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus.( 2009-Till date) Coordinator, ME Design Engineering for working professionals, BITS Pilani, Hyderabad Campus.(2012-13).
