WORK EXPERIENCE

EDUCATION

Thesis Title: Confinement induced ordering of polymer bilayer and blend thin films 
Advisor: Prof. Rabibrata Mukherjee 

 
M.Tech in Chemical Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur (July 2010 - April 2012)

Research Interest 










                                Research Interest and Research Group


Broad Research Areas:   Nanotechnology, Soft Lithography, Polymer Thin Film Instability, Polymer Blends, Superhydrophobic and self-cleaning surfaces. Current Research Activities: Development of superomniphobic surfaces for self-cleaning and antimicrobial applications Studying durability of hydrophobic surfaces.Studying polymer thin film instability in multi-component films and using soft lithography for spatial organization of nano-structuresAlignment and control of phase separation in polymer blendsStudying self assembly and spatial organization of biomoleculesResearch Group:  PhD Student:    Pichkari Saikiran (August 2019-till date) Topic: Instability mediated morphology modulation in thin polymer films ME Thesis Student: Sophia Patel (2019-2020), Topic: Theoretical and mathematical study on instability in pre-patterned thin films. For more details: SOFT NANOTECHNOLOGY LAB
