My Profile










                                Dr. Michael Alphonse


About the Faculty : Education : 1.Ph.D. in Mathematics from Indian Institute of Technology, Kanpur, Year of Completion :1993 2.M.E. in Software Systems from Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani, Year of Completion: 2002. Work Experience :  1. Visiting Scientist at I.S.I. Calcutta for 1 year 2. Post Doctoral Fellow at I.I.T., Kanpur for 2 years 3.  2 years of Industry Experience at Satyam Computers 4. 20 years of teaching and research experience at BITS-Pilani, in two disciplines Mathematics and Computer Science.
