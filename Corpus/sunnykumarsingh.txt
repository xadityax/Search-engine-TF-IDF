Profile










                                Dr. Sunny Kumar Singh


Assistant Professor,K-212, Department of Economics & Finance, Birla Institute of Technology & Science (BITS) Pilani, Hyderabad Campus, Jawahar Nagar,Shameerpet Mandal, Hyderabad - 500 078Telangana State, India Email: sunny.singh@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.inGoogle Scholar Profile: https://scholar.google.co.in/citations?user=GSfhybIAAAAJ&hl=enPhone: +91-40-66303698 Educational Qualifications2017: Fellow Program in Management (Ph.D.), Economics, Indian Institute of Management, Lucknow, India.           Thesis Title: Essays on Currency in Circulation           Thesis Advisor: Dr. Kaushik Bhattacharya 2010: M. A., Economics, Gokhale Institute of Politics & Economics, Pune, India. 2007: B. A., Economics, Indira Gandhi National Open University, New Delhi, India. Professional Experience  June 2016 - December 2016: Assistant Professor (Economics), Narsee Monjee Institute of Management Studies, Hyderabad, India July 2010 - May 2012: Academic Associate, Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad, India  Awards & Fellowship Received Highly Commendable Research Paper Award (jointly with Dr. Kaushik Bhattacharya) at the 2nd PAN IIM World Management Conference held at IIM Kozhikode during November 5-8, 2014. Awarded UGC-NET Certificate in Economics (December 2012).
