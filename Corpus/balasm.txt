 Profile





Hard work leads to MORE hard work










                                Mr. Balasubramanian. M


M.E. (Communication Engineering) Contact Details   Email: balasubramanian@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.inPhone: +91 9849821282 About the FacultyMr. Balasubramanian is working as a Lecturer in Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering, BITS-Pilani, Hyderabad campus since June, 2016. Prior to joining as lecturer, he was a full-time research scholar with CSIR-NET fellowship. Prior to joining BITS-Pilani, Hyderabad campus, he did M.E. (Communication Engineering) and B.E.(Hons) (Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering) from BITS-Pilani, Pilani campus in 2013 & 2010 respectively. He was recipient of the competitive BITSAA Travel Grant scholarship for presenting paper at Photonics Global Conference, 2012 held in Singapore by NTU, Singapore. His present research interest is in Optical MEMS based sensors for Inertial Navigation.
