Profile










                                Dr. Santanu Koley


Myself Dr. Santanu Koley, an Assistant Professor at the Dept. of Mathematics, Birla Institute of Science and Technology - Pilani,  Hyderabad Campus.Prior to join at BITS Pilani, I was having teaching and research experience as (i) INSPIRE Faculty at IISER Bhopal - 2018(ii) Assistant Professor at DA-IICT, Gandhinagar - 2017 to 2018 (iii) SERB - National Postdoc Fellow at IIT Ropar - 2016 to 2017(iv) Research Associate at IIT Kharagpur - 2015 to 2016 PhD - IIT Kharagpur (2016)MSC - IIT Kharagpur (2011) PhD and Postdoc with Individual Fellowships (CSIR/UGC-NET, NBHM, SERB-NPDF) can contact me through my email address: santanukoley1989@gmail.com,   santanu@hyderabad.bits-pilani.ac.in  Contact Details:Office Phone No:040-6630 3588
