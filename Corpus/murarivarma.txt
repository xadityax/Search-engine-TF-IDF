Academic Qualifications
 
1. Ph.D (Civil Engineering), Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, 2010.
 
2. M. E (Environmental Engineering), Bharathiar University, Government College of  Technology, Coimbatore, 1999.
 
3. B.Tech. (Civil Engineering),University of Calicut, Government Engineering College, Thrissur, Kerala,1996. 
 
Professional Experience
 
Visiting Faculty, Worley Parsons India, Hyderabad 05/2015 - 06/2015
 
Assistant Professor, Department of Civil Engineering, Thapar University, Patiala, 09/2010 - 01/2011
 
Project Assistant, Indo-French Cell for Water Sciences, Department of Civil Engineering, Indian Institute of Science, Bangalore, 03/2007 - 09/2010
